HubBox - Collect Point App

The app is built for the shops staff users from HubBox Collect Points locations and allows its users to perform tasks as follows:

  1. Adding parcels in the system
  2. Releasing parcels from the system
  3. On-board new Collect Points - demo the functionality of the app through a 'features guide tour' menu which helps staff from Collect Points to engage with the app and use it to ease the process of handling parcels
  4. Training new staff without involving a face to face meeting
  5. Keeps the users informed with push notifications about the upcoming parcels
  6. Notifying the users when their packages are ready to be collected
