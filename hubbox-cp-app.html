HubBox - Collect Point App

An app meant for all the HubBox Collect Points to add and release parcels into the system, on-board new Collect Points and train the staff without having to meet everyone face to face, engaging them using push notifications to keep them informed about the upcoming parcels or customers who just signed in to collect their parcels into their shop. Some important contributions:

  1. Deliver an easy to use app without complicated features so everyone can use it
  2. Push notifications to keep Collect Points engaged
  3. New Relic – for Analytics and Crash reports
  4. Floating Action Buttons for each screen with a quick “How to” video
  5. Keeping them logged in into the app, re-authenticate and finish the request without the user’s notice
  6. Permanently keeping an eye for crash reports and quick fixing them (2 Crash reports in 5 months)
  7. Testing the App, asking for feedback from other colleagues and Collect Points to meet their demands.
  8. Design compatibility with different screen sizes and orientations.
  9. Code migration from swift 2.2 to swift 3 and bug fixing
